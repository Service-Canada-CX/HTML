Privacy Notice Statement

About Our Survey

The Department of Employment and Social Development Canada wants to hear from young Canadians in Canada (age 15 – 30).

We want to know how you feel about sharing information about yourself with the Government of Canada. Your answers will help us improve how we deliver online services to young Canadians.

We also want to know if you would like to participate in a 60-minute interview. This interview will help us understand how you feel about sharing information with the Government of Canada, and why.

If you are 18 years of age or older, and you want to participate in the interview, we will ask you for the following information:

  • Your name and email address. This will allow us to contact you for an interview.
  • Some information about you, such as your level of education. This will help us select interview candidates with a diverse set of experiences.

Not everyone who volunteers will be selected for an interview. If you are selected, you will receive an email with more information from: edsc.jeunesse-youth.esdc@servicecanada.gc.ca.

Participation is Voluntary

The survey and interview are voluntary. There are no consequences if you choose not to participate. You can withdraw your participation at any time throughout the process.

Your Privacy is Protected

We’ve taken steps to protect your privacy and data. We will:

  • only collect information required for this survey and interview
  • keep your information safe
  • keep accurate records
  • not use your information to make any decisions about you or your family
  • never share your information without your explicit permission

 You have ways to protect your information. You can at any time:

  • Ask to view your information
  • Ask to change your information
  • Complain if you don’t think we’re handling your information appropriately

You can contact the Youth Digital Gateway Team if you have questions or concerns: edsc.jeunesse-youth.esdc@servicecanada.gc.ca.

If you have a complaint about how your information is handled by ESDC, you can file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner here:

https://www.priv.gc.ca/faqs/index_e.asp#005

Legal framework

The personal information you provide is collected under the authority of the Department of Employment and Social Development Act.

Your personal information is handled following the Privacy Act, the Department of Employment and Social Development Act and other applicable laws. 

Your personal information is described in the Personal Information Bank, Outreach Activities (PSU 938).  You can learn more about how to access this information by visiting the Government of Canada website  Information about programs and information holdings.  You can also visit a Service Canada Centre to access this information.

Contact Us

You can contact the Youth Digital Gateway Team if you have questions or concerns: edsc.jeunesse-youth.esdc@servicecanada.gc.ca.
